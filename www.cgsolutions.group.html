The CGS Story

Developing and Supplying Fixtures for the World

June 2019

Foundation

CGS was founded by Knud Clausen in 2019. After many year of partnerships in the Fit Out industries, Knud decided it was time to go it on his own. With an experienced team he set up offices and manufacturing facilities in Jiading, an industrious suburb of Shanghai.

2020

Moving to new Premises

After only a short time in the original office, due to expansion CGS had to move to larger premises in Shanghai. CGS started working in many new areas and have successfully begun work in the FF&E and hospitality industries as well as Retail fit-outs. 

2021

Now

This year has so far been a blockbuster, we have expanded and opened sales offices in both Dubai and Vietnam. Vietnam will also be a sourcing office for CGS, increasing the value we can add to our clients.
